About Us

Extreme Electronics is a team of engineers who design and build end-to-end solutions spanning AI/ML, Electronics, and Mechatronics. From concept through deployment, we bring together software intelligence, custom hardware, and mechanical systems to solve real-world problems.

AI/ML

Computer vision, classification models, intelligent control systems, and custom AI software that gives hardware the ability to perceive and adapt in real time.

Electronics

Custom PCB design, sensor integration, power management, IoT connectivity, PLC/SCADA programming, and control panel engineering.

Mechatronics

Mechanical design with embedded control — robotic arms, autonomous vehicles, conveyor systems, and precision machines that operate reliably in the field.
